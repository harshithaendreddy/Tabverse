Privacy Policy

TabVerse does not collect or share any personally identifiable information.

This extension uses chrome.storage.sync to save tab group data locally in your browser, and sync it across your own devices via your Google account. This data is never sent to any external server, and is only accessible by the extension.

We do not use analytics, tracking, or ad services of any kind.
